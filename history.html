The History of Computers

Early Beginnings

The history of computers dates back to ancient times with simple counting tools like the abacus. In the 1800s, Charles Babbage designed the Analytical Engine, considered the first concept of a general-purpose computer.

The 20th Century

In the 1940s, the first electronic computers like ENIAC and UNIVAC were built. The invention of the transistor in 1947 revolutionized computing, making computers smaller and more powerful.

Modern Era

Today, computers are everywhere, from desktops and laptops to smartphones and smart devices. The internet, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence continue to shape the future of technology.
